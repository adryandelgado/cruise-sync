Always lead the recommended option first and mark it clearly. Do not present all choices as equally weighted — pick the right next step and default to it.

**Why:** User explicitly corrected me for presenting auth, CSPO page, and inventory as a flat list without a recommendation, when the obvious right next step (auth) should have been called out.

**How to apply:** When offering next-step choices, say "Recommended: X — because Y" first. List alternatives briefly below it. Or just build the recommended thing and say what comes after it.
